My role

Principal Service Designer

Scale

2.2M children · 466K families

The systemic problem

Millions of families eligible for free school meals and childcare funding were not receiving what they were entitled to because the checking infrastructure was fragmented, manual and opaque.

What was at stake

Not a digital product alone, but a national entitlement system where design failure meant children going hungry and families unable to access childcare they had a legal right to receive.

The design challenge

Connect government data sources, policy frameworks, operational complexity and real-time eligibility checks into a service that works clearly and reliably for everyone it touches.
